About

Gastown is not like the usual places in Vancouver. Gastown has all the "hip" restaurants and clothing stores are. If you are looking for a place to spend at, I would definitely recommend Gastown. You can do some shopping and when you get tired, there are a lot of cafes with great interior design. There are a lot of shops that sell great accessories, furnitures, and so on.
