Comments & Editor's Note

The reason why I made this website is because Gastown is pretty much the only place I love in Vancouver. When I was living in Vancouver in my high school days, I was not so of an outgoing student. After taking some time off in my home country, I had to come back to Vancouver and I had hard time getting used to the environment again because I could not find any places that I can enjoy. My main interest was in fashion and music and I couldn't find a place to enjoy my hobbies.

Fortunately, I found Gastown where it has all the well-known clothing shops. The interior design and the clothes of the shops were very inspiring to me. The people there were also lively, from the locals to tourists. Therefore, I wanted to share my experience with others who are not used to Gastown or with tourists who want to see some good clothing stores in Vancouver.
